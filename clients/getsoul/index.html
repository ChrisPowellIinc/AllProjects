About Us

Don Coqui presents Get Soul, where we now feature the best of both worlds. Two home style menus - classic soul food and Authentic Latin Cuisine.

The Culture

Now you can enjoy our famous paella, chicken and waffles, roast pork, henny wings, shrimp with cheese grits, oxtail, fried catfish, mofongo, and so much more. The menu combines comfort dishes with mindful options for gluten Free diners and vegetarians.

Classic and specialty cocktails, craft beer, American wines, henney coladas, classic mojitos, and our famous sangrias are served by our Get Soul team to compliment your experience.

Got Soul?...Get Soul, Enjoy your favorite artist from Luther, Teddy, Drake, Beyonce, J.Lo, and Fat Joe just to name a few. We can't wait to see you! Where you Eat Drink and PARTY LIKE A ROCKSTAR! Open 7 Days a week!
